# QENEX LAB: The Engine for Civilization Acceleration

## Mission

To accelerate human civilization by automating the scientific method across all critical domains of progress: **Energy, Biology, Materials, and Physics.**

## Core Philosophy

1.  **Sovereignty**: Science must be open, local, and owned by humanity (User-controlled).
2.  **Rigor**: Every discovery must be validated by formal proofs (Q-Lang).
3.  **Universality**: The Trinity Pipeline (Theorize -> Generate -> Validate) applies to _all_ physical systems.

## The Universal Discovery Protocol

We replace "Trial and Error" with "Compute and Prove".

1.  **Define**: Express the physical goal in Q-Lang (e.g., `efficiency > 25%`).
2.  **Search**: AI explores 10^60 chemical space.
3.  **Prove**: Physics engine validates thermodynamic viability.
